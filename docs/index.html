Privacy Policy for Stocked

Last updated: December 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Stocked ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Device Permissions

The App requires certain permissions to function correctly:

  • Camera: We use the camera to scan barcodes of food products and to take photos of products for your inventory.
  • Photo Library: We access your photo library to allow you to select existing photos for your products.

1.2. Data Storage

Stocked uses Supabase, a cloud-based backend service, to store and synchronize your inventory data, shopping lists, and preferences across devices. Your data is stored securely in the cloud.

1.3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect information:

  • Supabase: We use Supabase for authentication and data storage. Please refer to Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenFoodFacts: We query the OpenFoodFacts database to retrieve product information when you scan a barcode. Please refer to OpenFoodFacts' Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. We have configured the App to request non-personalized ads. However, AdMob may still collect and use your device's advertising ID and other data for frequency capping, aggregated ad reporting, and to combat fraud and abuse. You can manage your ad preferences in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality (e.g., scanning barcodes, managing inventory, syncing data across devices).
  • Facilitate workspace sharing with other users.
  • Display advertisements (non-personalized).
  • Improve the App's performance and user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with third-party service providers (like Supabase and Google AdMob) as described in section 1.3 to facilitate the services they provide.

If you choose to share a workspace with other users, the data within that workspace (inventory items, shopping lists) will be visible to and editable by those users.

4. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

5. Account Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data.

5.1. In-App Deletion

You can delete your account and all data directly within the App:

  1. Open the Stocked app.
  2. Go to the Settings tab.
  3. Scroll down to the Danger Zone.
  4. Tap Wipe Data and confirm the action.

This will immediately delete your account, authentication data, and all inventory data associated with your user ID from our servers.

5.2. Manual Deletion Request

If you are unable to access the App, you may request account deletion by emailing us at schmidt@sdeveloper.cz with the subject "Account Deletion Request". Please include your registered email address. We will process your request and delete your data within 30 days.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
